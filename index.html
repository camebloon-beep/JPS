What We Do

Our Services

We care about your garden and your home. Here is how we can help you keep your property beautiful.

Gardening & Landscaping

Complete garden design, planting, lawn mowing, and yard tidy-ups to make your garden look just like you: clean and beautiful.

Tree Felling

Safe dismantling, felling, and control cutting of dangerous trees. We protect your home, roof, and fences during operation.

Palm Tree Trimming

Grooming and cleaning palm trunks, removing heavy dry dead fronds, loose seeds, and berries for a sleek, tidy tropical appeal.

Stump Grinding & Removal

Dismantling heavy tree stumps. We grind old tree roots and stumps below ground level to clear paths for new planting or landscaping.

Tree Pruning & Topping

Professional tree trimming to encourage sunlight flow and robust growth. Keeps branches safe from encroaching power lines.

Rubble & Waste Removal

We clean up completely after all tree felling and gardening work, removing all branches, logs, and garden refuse.
